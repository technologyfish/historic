Word on the street.

Discover the history all around you! There are three ways to get involved

Guided History Tours in Mercersburg

Join me, Jolie Zhu, for a 45-minute tour of Mercersburg, designed just for kids! We'll explore the streets, buildings, and stories that make our town special.

Email wots.org@gmail.com for upcoming tour dates!

Online History Sessions

Can't make it in person?
No problem!
Join our online sessions where
we introduce Word on the Street,
teach why learning local histories is important,
and do fun activities. Kids can explore history
in their own homes by finding objects around
them that tell a story about their neighborhood

Email wots.org@gmail.com
for online session info!

Activity Worksheets & Interactive Map

Want to dig even deeper? Download our activity worksheet to explore your own local history. Once filled out, your contributions can be added to our map by emailing wots.org@gmail.com to share a photo of your worksheet! Click on different parts of the map to see what other kids have discovered about their local history!
